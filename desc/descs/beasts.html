CYCLOP

The rarest and strongest of the primates. They have a deeper connection with the spirits than they do with any other beings, and have a deep respect for the delicate balance of nature. The only contact they have with other cyclops is for reproduction: and even then, the mother stays raising the child by herself for another 45 years, whenever it is finally ready to look after itself and its own areas. The lifespan of an average cyclop is around 900 years old. In all this lifetime, they are able to learn to speak with nature itself; and work with its powers and influences.

MUSHROOMS

Mushrooms are an extremely diverse separate world of species and beasts. They could be anywhere from a hive minded monster; to an independant being doing its share to keep the balance. They could have endless possibilities of skills, when you take into account the immense genetic pool they keep drawing from. All shrooms have the ability to connect to the M-NET, and also have high engaging in Mutualism.

M-NET

The myscelium net that connects 90% of rooted beings on most major biomes, constantly exchanging information between plants, fungi, and even some species of insects.
M-NET STILL HAS LARGELY UNKNOWN AND UNSTUDIED POTENTIAL!

Blue mushrooms are benevolent and intelligent mushrooms. Acting as a hive when close-by, mushrooms will often go 'rogue' or 'independant' when they, respectively, go on missions either personal or for the hive. They can also, as most mushrooms can, connect to the M-NET.
